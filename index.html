About

What is WastExchange ?

WastExchange is for the collection of unwanted wastes like old papers, cardboards, plastics, metal scraps, E-waste from the households, local collectors etc.

Convenient

WastExchange is easy to use and service oriented.

Transparent

WastExchange is honest and open when communicating with stakeholders.

Sustainable

WastExchange is sustainable, supportive and eco-friendly.

Economical

The market for WastExchange is generating significant amount of benefits to user.
