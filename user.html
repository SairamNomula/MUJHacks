What is WastExchange ?

WastExchange is for the collection of unwanted wastes like old papers, cardboards, plastics, metal scraps, E-waste from the households, local collectors etc.

What we accept?

Paper

Old newspapers, Old books

Metal

Unwanted metals, Rusted metals.

Plastic

Old plastic toys, bottles etc.

E-waste

Unwanted and old electronic gadgets.
